# Adopters of AGE Observability Specification

This file lists the organizations, projects, and industrial systems that adopt the **AGE Observability Specification** to instrument and audit their Artificial General Engineering (AGE) systems.

## Production Adopters

* **[PhysiTrace](https://github.com/vitalf/physitrace)**: Uses the AGE Observability semantic conventions as its core "Flight Data Recorder" mechanism to map ML decisions, kinematics simulations, and operator adjustments to raw edge controller anomalies on milling and robotic assembly lines.

## Tools & Libraries

* **[AGE Fluent Bit Log Decorator](https://github.com/vitalf/physitrace)**: An edge agent utility that correlation-injects `trace_id` and `span_id` context into Modbus, OPC-UA, and MTConnect industrial log lines.
